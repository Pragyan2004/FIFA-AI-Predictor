Cookie Policy

We use cookies to improve your experience.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us recognize your device and remember your preferences and session data.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the platform to function securely (e.g., login sessions).
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how users interact with the dashboard to improve UI/UX.
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings, such as your preferred tournament view or selected timezone.

3. Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling essential cookies may severely impact your ability to use the AI prediction tools.
